About Me

Richard Whitehead

Richard is from Hampton, VA. He enlisted in the US Army January 1997 as a Chemical, Biological, Radiologicl, Nuclear (CBRN) specialist with a focus on biometrics, forensics, and Weapons of Mass Destruction. As an enlisted Soldier he earned a Master's in of Public Health and became a Commisioned Officer. One of his main goals is to become a software developer and form a non-profit to teach inner city youth coding. Richard is married and has four kids, three girls (Naja, Amaya, Makkiya) and one son (Elijah). Richard enjoys playing basketball, weightlifting, and sky diving.
